## Opinions on LLMs

I think LLMs are just _okay_. I can acknowlegde that they are useful as they find solutions to difficult problems quickly, but that does not mean that the solution they generate are without flaws. It is up to the user to do the work and verify if the LLM is actually providing quality, rather than mumbo jumbo.

It is also improtant to note that LLMs should not replace thinking and effort. We should actually use our brains before resorting to the use of AI, as I feel that it is very easy to fall into the trap of constantly using LLMs due to the convience. Many that fall into the trap do not realize that they are deskilling themselves, making it harder for them to solve problems on their own. 

Also, it is really annoying how LLms are being shoved in our faces. Why does almost every website have a virtual assistant now???
